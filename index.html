about

I run KuratorPro, a career-document platform, alongside a small agency offering CV, cover letter, and coaching services — so I end up designing the tool and using it, in the same week.

Most days are spent in Spring Boot and MongoDB: modelling how a person's career maps onto a document, generating clean PDFs at scale, and keeping a self-hosted stack running on modest hardware without it falling over. Outside of that, I write about business ideas and occasionally help prepare research presentations on economics topics.
